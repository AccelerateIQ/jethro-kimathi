About Jethro

A Man of Faith,
Land & Purpose

Jethro Kimathi is a minister, entrepreneur, and agribusiness practitioner based in Kenya. His life's work sits at the intersection of spiritual leadership, sustainable farming, and business development — driven by the conviction that faith must produce fruit in every dimension of life.

Through preaching, teaching, and community engagement, Jethro builds up individuals and congregations across Kenya and beyond. In the field, he demonstrates that modern agribusiness and Kingdom values are not in conflict — they are partners.

His vision: a generation of Africans who are spiritually grounded, economically empowered, and deeply rooted in the land God has given them.
